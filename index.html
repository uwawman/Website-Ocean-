About

This program is about saving the ocean from several problems like trash littering, cases of poisoning sea water, mass fish hunting, and other problems. Why do we want to do this program? It's because we are lacking of clean water and healthy fish and we need to clean and save the ocean from problems so we could reach our achievement together.
